Who are we?

History

IDEAAS was founded by Federico Berón, a young developer, leader and entrepreneur from Mendoza, Argentina.
While working as a university professor, he noticed that the only way to make a living in Bolívar as a developer was to either be lucky enough to land a remote gig or move to a bigger city.
Unhappy with the recurring situation, he decided he would use the experience and contacts he had acquired over the years to help his students advance their careers by providing them with a co-working space, guidance and real-life project work.
Eventually, as the project grew, so did his goals.
Today, more than 10 people work at IDEAAS. Moreover, we are part of "Polo Tecnológico de Bolívar", which is an association whose creation and development was also encouraged by Federico.
